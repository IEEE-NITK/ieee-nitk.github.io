About I2CONECCT

IEEE Mangalore Subsection and IEEE NITK Student Branch presents I2CONECCT 2022 (Industry Institute Conclave on Electronics, Computational and Communication Technologies), an intercollegiate project exhibition and competition.

Rules and Regulations

  • Registrations to be rolled out by 30th May 2022 via all social media platforms.
  • Rs.300 per team to register (Rs.100 concession upon submitting proof of IEEE Student Membership for atleast one member of the team)
  • Teams of size 2-6 members, open to all final year B.Tech/BE/M.Tech/BCA/MCA students in all branches of study across Karnataka.
  • A maximum of four submissions allowed per institute.
  • E-certificates will be distributed to all participants.

Project Shortlisting & Evaluation

Each track will shortlist 8 projects. In the process of shortlisting, any information related to the team and institute will not be disclosed to maintain transparency. The organizing committee will ensure that projects from each track are shortlisted fairly and without any bias. Projects will be shortlisted on the following criteria:

      1. Relevance of the project
      2. Application and implementation of project
      3. Technical complexity and correctness of the project

Shortlisted teams will be evaluated by IEEE-MSS members from academia and industry. The organizing committee will create panels to evaluate the projects based on the membersâ€™ areas of expertise.
