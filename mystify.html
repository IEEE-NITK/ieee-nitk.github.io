What is Mystify?

“ The art of simplicity is a puzzle of complexity ”

Have you ever been to an escape room? You know how expensive it can get for an hour of fun. But what if you can have the fun of uncovering clues and demystifying puzzles for free over a period of 2 days. IEEE NITK presents the 3rd edition of Mystify!. The first edition of this unique, enthralling and mind-boggling puzzle competition to be held offline, we introduce a new format of puzzles as well. The only escape you'll be doing here is from your monotonous lives.

It's open to all branches of 1st & 2nd year students of NITK.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who is eligible to participate in Mystify?

All 1st and 2nd year students from all branches at NITK are welcome to participate in Mystify.

Is there a cost associated with registration?

No, there is no registration fee.

Do I need any prior experience or knowledge to participate?

No prerequisites are necessary to participate.

Is Mystify similar to Capture The Flag (CTF)?

No, Mystify is not a CTF event. Participants in Mystify are challenged to create puzzles for others to solve, rather than finding and exploiting vulnerabilities in systems. Both Mystify and CTF involve problem solving, but they have different objectives and formats.

How many people can be in a team?

Teams are limited to a maximum of four members.

Do I need to be an IEEE member or have at least one IEEE member on my team to participate?

IEEE membership is not a requirement for participation, and having an IEEE member on your team is not necessary.

How should I prepare for Mystify?

Come with an open mind, embrace creativity, and be ready to have fun!

How are teams selected for Round 2?

The top 50% of teams will advance to Round 2.

Can I receive a participation certificate by only clearing Round 1?

No, to receive a participation certificate, you must solve at least 2 puzzles in Round 2. Simply clearing Round 1 is not enough.

What equipment or materials do I need to bring to the event?

Each team must have one laptop (Windows, MacOS or Linux) and basic stationery like notepads and pens is advised. Teams may bring more than one laptop if they choose to do so.

Can I participate in Round 2 from a remote location?

Yes, you can participate in Round 2 from wherever you are, but participants on site will receive Onesta coupons (and more incentives may be added later).
